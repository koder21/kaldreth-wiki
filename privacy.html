Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Kaldreth mobile game (the "Game"), published by Pseudo Playgrounds ("we", "us"), handles information when you play. By installing or playing the Game you agree to the practices described here.

Kaldreth is an offline-first idle RPG. Your game progress is stored on your device and, if you choose to sign in, in your Google Play cloud save. We do not operate our own game servers and do not maintain user accounts.

Information We and Our Partners Process

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game offers optional rewarded ads. Ads are provided by Google AdMob, which may collect and process device and advertising identifiers (including your Advertising ID), IP address, and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland we present a consent request before personalized ads are used; you can change or withdraw your choice at any time via Settings → Privacy → Manage Ad Privacy in the Game. See Google's Privacy & Terms and the AdMob data disclosure.

Google Play Games Services

If you sign in, Google Play Games Services provides authentication and cloud saving of your characters. This involves your Google Play player identifier and the save data you generate. Sign-in is optional; the Game is fully playable without it. See Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

Purchases (such as the Warden's Mark) are processed by Google Play Billing. We receive confirmation that a purchase was made so we can grant the item; we never receive or store your payment card or billing details.

Bug Reports

If you use the in-app "Report a Bug" feature, the text you write, the app version, and your operating-system name are sent through Formspree so we can read and act on your report. Please do not include personal information in bug descriptions.

Local Game Data

Your characters, progress, settings, and purchase entitlement are stored locally on your device (save files are encrypted). Uninstalling the Game removes local data; deleting a character removes that character's data.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • We do not collect precise location.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not run our own analytics or tracking servers.

How Information Is Shared

Information is processed only by the service providers described above (Google AdMob, Google Play Games Services, Google Play Billing, and Formspree for bug reports), each acting under its own privacy policy. We do not share data with any other third parties except where required by law.

Children

Kaldreth is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will address it.

Data Retention & Your Choices

  • Ad consent: change or withdraw it in Settings → Privacy → Manage Ad Privacy.
  • Local data: delete a character in-game, or uninstall the Game to remove all local data.
  • Cloud saves: managed through your Google Play account; you may delete Game data from Google Play Games.
  • Advertising ID: you can reset or delete it in your device's Google settings.
  • Requests: to ask about or request deletion of data we hold, contact us below.

Security

Local save files are encrypted on your device. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this Policy or your data can be sent to admin.kaldreth@gmail.com.
